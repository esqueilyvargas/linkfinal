Reproductive Health

Consent -is an AGREEMENT between two of age, enthusiastic individuals before engaging in a sexual act. Consent CAN be withdrawn at any time. Maybe IS NOT consent. Example of consent: "Do you want to do this with me?... Yes, do you? Yes. = CONSENT! Do you want to do this with me?... No." = NOT consent --No means no-- Consent is important in any relationship, romantic or platonic. It gives you a right to refuse physical or psychological attention at any time. It should be present eveywhere from holding hands to having sex. Consent can be withdrawn at any moment and that decision should be respected regardless of the situation. When discussing consent with a friend or partner it is important to respect each other and understand that no means no.

Every man and woman should have access to reliable birth control. There are several methods of birth control to think about when deciding how to protect yourself. Condoms are a popular choice for many people, but that doesn't work for everyone. So whether you use the pill, an IUD, or an implant remember that a condom is the only way to protect you and your partner from STDs.

Abortion - the decision of abortion is up to the person thinking about receiving it. LINK believes that individuals should have the freedom to choose. Abortion laws in each state are different and we recommend looking at the GET CARE page for reliable information about abortion legislation and restrictions.
